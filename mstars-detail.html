Mystars

Stories make us human

MyStars provides a place for job candidates to present their true selves to potential employers, to go beyond the resume and control when and how their professional profiles are available. Recruiters care who you are beyond the facts and figures on a resume. That’s why they research candidates’ digital footprints. Get ahead of the Googling and show your full and dynamic persona right up front. Tell the stories and show the results behind the facts and figures on your resume.
